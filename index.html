  • Statement of Faith

    Hounslow Christian Fellowship church is about connecting people to God, the Church is full of hope for salvation which is free gift of God to all ,who have discovered an incredible richness of life that comes from having a personal relationship with Jesus Christ . We believe that God changes lives through His Word the Bible and our goal is to see lives transformed through expositional, biblical and passionate preaching and teachings by the inspiration of holy spirit of God.

    Hounslow Christian fellowship Church accepts the Scriptures as the revealed will of God, and adopts these statements of Fundamental Truths and Doctrine. The Bible is the inspired Word of God and the revealed will of God (II Tim. 3:15-17; I Peter 1:23-25; Heb. 4:12).

    There is one true Godhead comprised of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. All are co-equal and co-eternal (Mt. 28:19, Jn. 1:1, 14). Man was created in God’s image. By voluntary transgression he fell and his only hope of redemption is in Jesus Christ, the Son of God (Gen. 1:26-31, 3:1-7; Rom. 5:12-21).

Welcome to HounSlow FellowShip Church

Hounslow Christian Fellowship (HCF) is a local church based on the principles of the New Testament. It is a non-denominational, multicultural Church patterned after the practices of Early Church in Jerusalem (Acts 2:42).

We are devoted to the Word of God (Bible) and to fellowship, to the breaking of bread and to Prayer. We believe that Jesus Christ died for all and anyone can be saved by faith in Jesus and by testifying through Water Baptism. We believe that all believers are equal being the members of the body and subjected to the headship of Lord Jesus Christ.

We have Holy Communion every Sunday. We meet regularly for Bible Study and Prayer during the week. It is a family church that ministers to adults, youth and children. We have weekly Sunday School specially focused for upbringing Children in the fear and knowledge of God. Special activities for children and youth.

We hearty wlecome you to come and join us for fellowship every Sunday at 10:30 AM in Hounslow, Greater London, UK. Please follow the links to learn more about our ministries and programs. We would love to hear from you if you have any prayer or need please contact us by using the Contact Us link. Thank you for visiting our Website.
